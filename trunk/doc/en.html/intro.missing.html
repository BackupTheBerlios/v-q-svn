1.4. Missing features

Things that are popular but missing in v-q:

  • support POP3 before SMTP relaying (vpopmail does have such thing) - people use SMTP-AUTH!

  • support real users, authorization based on /etc/passwd

  • support for any other mailboxes type than Maildir

  • quota support
